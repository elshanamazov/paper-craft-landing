Maximum whiteness

To increase whiteness, smoothness and softness, white mineral substances are added to the paper pulp: chalk, talc, kaolin, etc. This operation is called filling.

The paper sheet is cast on a paper-making machine, the most important part of which is a continuously moving (like a conveyor) metal or nylon mesh.
